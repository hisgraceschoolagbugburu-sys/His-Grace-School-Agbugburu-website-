What You Need

Admission Requirements

Ensure all credentials are fully prepared before submitting your application package to avoid processing delays.

Mandatory Credentials

  • Passport Photograph: Two recent passport-sized color photographs of the candidate (white background), and one photograph of each parent or statutory guardian.
  • Birth Certificate: A clear photocopy and original copy (for verification) of the student's Birth Certificate or official statutory age declaration.
  • Previous School Result: Last terminal academic report card or cumulative academic history records from the candidate's previous school (where applicable).
  • Parent or Guardian Information: Fully completed emergency contacts, home/office addresses, identification (NIN or Driver's license), and legal relationship declarations.
  • Completed Admission Form: The official registration form fully filled, signed by the parent or statutory guardian, containing signed codes of conduct.

Submission Guidelines

All files must be clear and legible. If submitting physical photocopies at our campus, please enclose them inside a neat plastic envelope labeled with the candidate's full name.

For candidates entering primary grades, a brief math, spelling, and reading evaluation is conducted immediately to facilitate optimal grade level placing.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find immediate answers regarding terms, timelines, and registration workflows below.

When is admission open?

Admissions at His Grace School Agbugburu are open year-round for new pupils, subject to vacancy constraints. However, we highly recommend commencing the application process at least 4-6 weeks prior to the beginning of the targeted academic term to ensure seamless placement and onboarding.

How do I apply?

You can begin the process entirely online by clicking the "Apply for Admission" button on this page, which will guide you to our registration form (applicant-register.html). Alternatively, you can visit the school administrative office in Agbugburu Village to purchase a physical application booklet.

How will I know if I am admitted?

Once all documents are submitted and any required baseline placement interaction is completed, our Admissions Committee reviews the file. You will receive an official notification via your registered email address and phone call within 3 to 5 business days detailing the admission status.

Can I edit my application later?

Yes. After registering on our portal, you will be able to save your application as a draft, close it, and log back in to edit or complete your information at any time prior to clicking final submit.
