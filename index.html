Nurturing Greatness

Dedicated to Academic Excellence & Holistic Development

His Grace School Agbugburu is a premier private educational institution located in the serene environment of Agbugburu Village, Odeda Local Government, Abeokuta, Ogun State, Nigeria. Our institution is deeply committed to providing an exceptional learning experience that seamlessly integrates rigorous academic excellence with sound moral training, high discipline, and spiritual grounding.

We believe that every child is endowed with unique seeds of greatness. Through our child-centric and holistic approach to learning, we nurture the cognitive, physical, moral, and social dimensions of our pupils. Our ultimate goal is to shape well-rounded individuals who possess the intellectual depth, moral integrity, and leadership capacity required to thrive and make positive contributions to our modern global society.

Admission into His Grace School Agbugburu is open to all qualified young learners who desire exceptional academic stimulation and strong moral grooming. We ensure a transparent, fair, and welcoming entry process for all applicants.

Step-by-Step Pathway

Our Admission Procedures

Please follow these steps carefully to secure a successful registration for your child.

1

1. Portal Registration / Inquiry

Initiate the registration process by clicking "Apply for Admission" below, or visit the main administrative building in Agbugburu Village to pick up an physical inquiry brochure.

2

2. Form Purchase & Submission

Acquire the termly admission application form, complete all required fields with accurate medical, contact, and scholastic details, and return it with supporting documents.

3

3. Entrance Assessment

The candidate will undergo a basic academic evaluation (written and interactive tests) to determine their current logical, mathematical, and linguistic capabilities.

4

4. Review & Decision

The Academic Advisory Board reviews results and informs parents regarding successful placement decisions within 3 to 5 business days.

5

5. Registration & School Fees

Upon acceptance, finalize payment of development school fees, purchase academic uniforms, collect booklist packs, and welcome your child to their first day of class!

Documents Checklist

Admission Requirements

Please make sure you have the following credentials ready for submission during your visit or upload phase:

  • Original and photocopy of the candidate's Birth Certificate or statutory age declaration.
  • Two recent passport photographs (full face, white background) of the student.
  • One passport photograph of each parent or statutory legal guardian.
  • Last terminal or cumulative Academic Report Card from the candidate's previous school (where applicable).
  • Evidence of medical fitness, vaccination cards, or blood type data.
  • Fully completed and signed Admission Undertaking form.
Common Questions

Admission FAQs

What are the school fees for new pupils?

His Grace School Agbugburu is committed to keeping premium education highly accessible. Termly fees vary according to the child's academic grade. Please contact our administrative office directly or request a fee breakdown brochure during your registration phase.

At what age can a child start at His Grace?

We enroll children into our pre-school and early foundation classrooms starting from 2 years old, nursery class from 3 years, and primary levels from 5 years. We ensure classrooms are age-appropriate and safely supervised.

Is there entrance assessment for kindergarten?

Our assessment for early foundation levels is entirely non-stressful. It is a warm, interactive session with our early childhood educators to check communication patterns, physical coordination, and initial social readiness.

Do you offer school bus services to Abeokuta town?

We are currently establishing structured school bus routes for surrounding neighborhoods inside Odeda Local Government area. Route expansions and scheduling forms can be requested at the admin desk during enrollment.
